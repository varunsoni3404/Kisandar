CONTRACT AGREEMENT

This Contract Agreement is made on this 1st day of January 2024, between:

Farmer: Rajesh Kumar, son of Mohan Kumar, residing at Village Anandpur, District Patiala, Punjab, India (hereinafter referred to as the "Farmer").

Contractor: Vikram Singh, son of Arjun Singh, residing at 123, Industrial Area, Mohali, Punjab, India (hereinafter referred to as the "Contractor").

WHEREAS, the Farmer is engaged in agricultural activities and has a requirement for certain services related to farming.

AND WHEREAS, the Contractor possesses the necessary skills, expertise, and resources to provide the required services.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. Scope of Work
The Contractor agrees to provide the following services to the Farmer:
Land preparation (plowing, harrowing, etc.)
Sowing of seeds
Irrigation management
Pest and weed control
Harvesting of crops
2. Duration of Contract
This contract shall commence on January 15, 2024, and shall continue until the completion of the harvesting of the crops, estimated to be by April 30, 2024, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions of this agreement.
3. Compensation
The Farmer agrees to pay the Contractor a total amount of ₹50,000 for the services rendered.
Payment shall be made in the following installments:
₹20,000 upon signing of this contract
₹15,000 upon completion of sowing
₹15,000 upon completion of harvesting
4. Obligations of the Farmer
The Farmer shall provide the Contractor with access to the land and any necessary resources (seeds, fertilizers, etc.) required for the services.
The Farmer shall ensure that the land is free from any legal encumbrances.
5. Obligations of the Contractor
The Contractor shall perform the services with due diligence and in a professional manner.
The Contractor shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations while performing the services.
6. Termination
Either party may terminate this contract by giving a written notice of 15 days to the other party if there is a breach of any terms of this contract.
Upon termination, the Contractor shall be entitled to payment for services rendered up to the date of termination.
7. Indemnification
The Contractor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Farmer from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from the Contractor's performance of the services.
8. Governing Law
This contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
9. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this contract shall be settled amicably between the parties. If no resolution can be reached, the dispute shall be referred to arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
10. Miscellaneous
This contract constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings.
Any amendments to this contract must be in writing and signed by both parties.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have executed this Contract Agreement as of the date first above written.
